About Event Horizon

Event Horizon is a university astronomy and stargazing club where students explore the night sky, learn about space, and enjoy astronomy together in a simple and friendly way.

Mission

To help students learn astronomy, build curiosity about the universe, and create fun stargazing experiences on campus.

Vision

To become a welcoming student club where anyone can explore the cosmos, share ideas, and grow a lifelong interest in space.
